    Root Canal Experts Near you

    If you live in the Huntington Beach area and have a severe toothache, an abscess or swelling and tenderness on your gums, or have developed hot and cold sensitivity on a certain tooth, you should schedule an appointment with Noleen Dental for a dental exam to see if you are in need of a root canal. When the nerve of your tooth is affected by infection or decay, it can be quite painful and left untreated the afflicted tooth will most certainly die and need to be removed. Root canal therapy will save you from getting your tooth pulled and the need for Dental Implants to keep the rest of your teeth positioned properly. The thought of removing bacteria, decay, and damaged nerve tissue can be scary but don’t worry, Dr. Nguyen has been practicing for well over 15 years in the Orange County area and has a soft touch.     Dental Implants you Can Trust

    If you have been considering Dental Implants in Huntington Beach, Noleen Dental has more than fifteen years of experience with full-service implant dentistry. With modern technology, affordable pricing and financing at low monthly payments for those without dental insurance, there is no reason to let a missing tooth ruin your smile. Usually, the dental office will outsource surgical placement to a specialist, but at Noleen Dental, Dr. Noleen Nguyen is a placement specialist and will plan your implant surgery and place the implant post or posts into your jaw so that you do not have to deal with multiple offices for the surgery and construction of the prosthetic implant itself. With over 15 years of experience, the single implants at Noleen Dental are indistinguishable from the surrounding teeth. If you need bridge work done, implant-supported bridges are a healthy way to replace groups of teeth. Old-school bridge-work can negatively impact the bordering teeth, but the implant version is held in place, permanently and securely via implant posts in the jaw. If you have suffered from missing natural teeth for some time, relying on dentures, bone deterioration may have already occurred. In this situation, mini dental implants may be the best course of action. Regardless of your dental implant needs, the restoration and replacement teeth at Noleen Dental are created from tooth-colored porcelain and will build a beautiful, stronger smile.
